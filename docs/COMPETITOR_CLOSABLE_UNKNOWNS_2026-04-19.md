### PrimeLister
- **App Store:** `apps.apple.com/us/app/poshmark-bot-primelister/id6478108527` — "Poshmark Bot: PrimeLister" — Publisher: ByteBlast Digital LTD — Rating: 4.9 / 2.2K ratings — Free (subscription ~$25/mo) — Last updated: March 2026 v1.0.30 (T1)
- **Google Play:** Same package `primelister.posh.bot...` — Rating: 4.7 / 493 reviews — Downloads: 10K+ — Last updated: July 25, 2025 (T1)
- **Mobile vs web:** App is Poshmark automation only (share, relist, offer, follow). Cross-listing requires the Chrome extension on desktop. Mobile app is a companion tool, not full platform parity.
- **Target user:** Mobile-native Poshmark sellers wanting automation without desktop.

- **Google Play:** No native Android app.
- **"Crosslisting" app (id1622734136):** This is a separate indie product by Michael Chierchio / Posh Sidekick, NOT Crosslist.com. Rating: 3.5 iOS / 2.0 Android — 271 iOS ratings / 134 Android reviews — Downloads: 10K+ — Last updated: Aug 27, 2024. Priced $5.99–$99.99/mo in-app. (T1)
- **Mobile vs web:** Crosslist.com roadmap shows "Full Mobile Listing functionality" as In Progress (121 votes). Currently web + browser extension only.
- **Note:** "Full App Redesign" shipped June 2025 includes improved mobile stability — mobile parity is actively planned.

## Vendoo Enterprise Tier

- **URL:** `https://www.vendoo.co/enterprise/high-volume` (T1)
- **Standard enterprise plan:** $399/month (2 months free on annual)
- **Custom plan:** One-time inventory transfer $499–$1,299 (up to 5K–20K items) plus White Glove Listing Service
- **Features included:** 3 admin users ($27/seat additional), AI photo editing, bulk cross-listing, CSV bulk upload, AI item creation, consolidated picking lists, offline/online inventory sync, customer service desk access
- **Sales contact:** Google Calendar booking or contact form — consultation-based; no self-serve enterprise checkout
- **Named customers:** None disclosed. Site claims "170,000+ Scaled Businesses" and "$1.4B+ In GMV" across all tiers
- **Pricing leaks from third-party:** Capterra and SaaSHub show same tiers — no hidden pricing data found. Tekpon and GetApp confirm $399/mo enterprise floor. (T3)
- **Conclusion:** Enterprise tier pricing is now confirmed public at `vendoo.co/enterprise/high-volume`. It is not a "call for pricing" — the $399/mo Standard rate is published.
