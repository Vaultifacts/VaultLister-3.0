Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Applies to vaultlister.com and all subdomains

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser or device storage when you visit a website. VaultLister uses a combination of browser cookies and localStorage to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and — with your consent — measure how the app is used.

VaultLister does not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or sell your data to any third party. The only analytics tool we use is Google Analytics 4, and it is loaded only after you give consent.

Cookies We Use

Cookie / Key Type Purpose Duration
_ga Analytics (Google) Distinguishes unique visitors for Google Analytics 4 2 years
_ga_LXETN4PYRM Analytics (Google) Stores session state for the GA4 measurement stream 2 years
cookie_consent Functional (VaultLister) Stores your cookie preference (accepted or declined) so the banner is not shown again Persistent (localStorage)
vaultlister_state Functional (VaultLister) Stores authentication tokens and app preferences required to keep you signed in Persistent (localStorage)
vaultlister_sidebar_collapsed Functional (VaultLister) Remembers whether the navigation sidebar is collapsed or expanded Persistent (localStorage)

Google Analytics cookies (_ga and _ga_LXETN4PYRM) are only set after you click Accept on the cookie banner. If you click Decline, those cookies are never created and analytics remain in denied state.

Functional Cookies

The VaultLister localStorage keys (vaultlister_state, vaultlister_sidebar_collapsed, cookie_consent) are strictly necessary for the application to function. They are not transmitted to third parties and are stored only on your device.

How to Opt Out or Clear Cookies

You have several options to control or remove cookies:

  • Decline analytics on this site: Click Decline on the cookie banner. If you have already accepted, clear cookie_consent from your browser’s localStorage (Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage) and reload the page.
  • Google Analytics opt-out add-on: Install the official browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout to prevent Google Analytics from collecting data across all websites you visit.
  • Clear all VaultLister data: In your browser’s Developer Tools, go to Application → Local Storage → your domain, and delete the keys listed above. This will sign you out.
  • Browser cookie settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies globally via their privacy or settings menu. Note that blocking functional cookies may prevent VaultLister from working correctly.
