About VaultLister

Built by resellers, for resellers.

Our Mission

VaultLister is a multi-channel reselling platform that helps sellers list to 9+ marketplaces from one dashboard. We believe reselling should be simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone.

Founded in 2026 in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, we set out to build the tool we wished existed when we started reselling. No hidden fees, no per-listing charges, no bait-and-switch pricing.

What We Believe

💰

Zero-Cost First

A generous free tier that stays free forever. Premium features earn their price.

🛒

Reseller-First Design

Built by actual resellers who understand the daily grind of cross-listing, closet sharing, and offer management.

🔒

Privacy by Default

Your data stays yours. No selling user data, no advertising cookies, no tracking beyond what you opt into.

⚡

AI That Actually Helps

Smart listing generation, intelligent price suggestions, and photo tools that save real time — not gimmicks.

Our Team

A small team building big tools for resellers everywhere. We ship fast, listen to feedback, and obsess over the details that make reselling easier.
