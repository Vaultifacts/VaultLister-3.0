General

VaultLister is a multi-channel reselling platform that lets you list items across 9+ marketplaces simultaneously, manage your inventory in one place, and automate repetitive tasks like closet sharing and offer management. It's built for resellers who sell on Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, Depop, Grailed, Etsy, Shopify, Facebook Marketplace, and Whatnot.
VaultLister combines cross-listing, inventory management, automation bots, AI-powered listing generation, offer management, and sales analytics into a single platform — with no per-listing fees. Most tools charge per listing or lock key features behind higher tiers. VaultLister's flat subscription covers everything, including Playwright-powered automations and Claude AI assistance.
VaultLister supports Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, Depop, Grailed, Etsy, Shopify, Facebook Marketplace, and Whatnot. We're continuously adding new platforms — check the Roadmap for upcoming integrations.

Cross-Listing & Inventory

You create a listing once in VaultLister — photos, title, description, price, condition — and then choose which marketplaces to publish it to. VaultLister maps your listing data to each platform's required format and submits it via the platform's API or automation. When an item sells on any platform, VaultLister automatically marks it as sold and delist it from the others.
VaultLister monitors your sales in real time. When a sale is detected on any connected platform, the item's inventory status is updated to "Sold" and it is automatically delisted from all other platforms where it was active. This prevents double-selling.
Yes. VaultLister can import your existing active listings from connected marketplaces. Once imported, your items are managed through VaultLister's inventory and can be cross-listed to additional platforms.

Automations & Account Safety

VaultLister is designed with account safety as a core priority. Automation actions (like Poshmark closet sharing) respect each platform's rate limits and recommended usage patterns. Actions are spaced with appropriate delays and logged for review. That said, no third-party tool can guarantee zero risk — use automations responsibly and review each platform's terms of service.
Current automations include: Poshmark closet sharing (share your entire closet on a schedule), follow-back automation (auto-follow users who follow you), and offer management (automatically send offers to likers based on configurable rules). More automations are in development — see the Roadmap.

Privacy & Data

VaultLister stores your inventory data, listing content, and marketplace credentials (encrypted with AES-256-GCM) on secure servers hosted on Railway. We do not sell your data to third parties. Your marketplace credentials are never exposed in plain text. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
