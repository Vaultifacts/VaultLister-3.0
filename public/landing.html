Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my marketplace accounts?

API platforms (eBay, Etsy, Shopify) connect with a single OAuth click — you'll be redirected to authorize VaultLister. Bot platforms (Poshmark, Depop, Facebook, Whatnot) use your login credentials, which are stored encrypted and never shared.

Is VaultLister really free?

Yes. The Free plan is unlimited in time and includes up to 10 active listings. Paid plans unlock more listings, platforms, and automation runs.

Which marketplaces are supported?

VaultLister currently supports Poshmark, eBay, Depop, Shopify, Facebook Marketplace, and Whatnot — 6 platforms live now. Mercari, Grailed, and Etsy are coming soon.

What happens when an item sells?

VaultLister automatically reduces inventory and pauses or removes the listing on all other connected platforms within seconds.

Does it work with my existing listings?

Yes. You can import existing listings from supported platforms and sync them into your VaultLister inventory.

Do I need technical skills to set it up?

No. API platforms connect with a single OAuth click. Bot platforms require your login credentials, which are stored encrypted and never shared.
