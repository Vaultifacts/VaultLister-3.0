VaultLister is a cloud-hosted platform. Your data is stored securely on managed infrastructure. We do not sell your data, use advertising trackers, or share your information with third parties except as described in this policy.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how VaultLister ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the VaultLister platform and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@vaultlister.com.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve the Service:

  • Authenticating your identity and maintaining your session
  • Storing and displaying your inventory, listings, and sales records
  • Connecting to third-party marketplaces on your behalf using OAuth tokens
  • Running automations you configure (sharing, offer rules, follow-back)
  • Generating AI-powered listing suggestions when you request them
  • Calculating analytics, profit margins, and performance reports
  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access or abuse
  • Sending transactional notifications (e.g. sale alerts, sync errors)

We do not use your data to train AI models. Content submitted to AI features is processed in real time and is not stored for model training purposes beyond what Anthropic's own data policies govern (see Section 6).

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database on managed cloud infrastructure. We take the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a minimum cost factor of 12 — plaintext passwords are never stored
  • Marketplace OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM (authenticated encryption) before being written to the database
  • All authenticated API requests require a short-lived JWT access token (15-minute expiry) and a secure refresh token (7-day expiry)
  • Multi-factor authentication (TOTP) is available and encouraged for all accounts
  • All mutating requests (POST, PUT, PATCH, DELETE) require a CSRF token to prevent cross-site request forgery
  • Automation actions are logged to a local audit log file for accountability

We maintain the security of VaultLister's cloud infrastructure, including regular software updates, access controls, and network security measures.

5. Marketplace API Integrations

To enable cross-listing and automation features, VaultLister connects to third-party marketplace platforms using OAuth 2.0. When you authorize a marketplace connection:

  • We receive and store an OAuth access token and refresh token for that marketplace
  • These tokens are encrypted before storage and are used only to perform the operations you explicitly request
  • We do not share your marketplace credentials or tokens with any other party
  • You can revoke access to any connected marketplace at any time from your account settings or directly through the marketplace's own OAuth management page

Each marketplace platform has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data you submit through its API. We encourage you to review those policies for the platforms you connect.

When you use VaultLister to list items on Facebook Marketplace, our Chrome extension assists by pre-filling listing forms in your browser. This operates within your own browser session using your existing Facebook login. VaultLister does not access your Facebook credentials or store your Facebook authentication tokens.

AI Info

How VaultLister uses artificial intelligence — transparently.

Which AI does VaultLister use?

VaultLister uses Claude, the AI assistant made by Anthropic. Claude powers our AI listing generation, price suggestion, image analysis, and Vault Buddy chat features.

Powered by Anthropic Claude

What AI features does VaultLister offer?

  • AI Listing Generation — Generates marketplace-optimised titles, descriptions, and keywords based on your item details and photos.
  • Price Suggestions — Analyses sold comparables across platforms and suggests competitive pricing for your items.
  • Image Analysis — Identifies item attributes, condition indicators, and categories from uploaded photos to pre-fill listing fields.
  • Vault Buddy Chat — A conversational AI assistant that answers reselling questions, helps with pricing strategy, and guides you through VaultLister features.

What data is sent to AI services?

When you use an AI feature, only the data needed for that task is sent to Anthropic's API:

  • For listing generation: your item title, description, condition, and category
  • For image analysis: uploaded item photos (processed as images)
  • For price suggestions: item details and category (no personal information)
  • For Vault Buddy: your chat messages

What is never sent to AI: your marketplace credentials, payment information, account passwords, or personal identity details.

Data retention and Anthropic's privacy policy

Data sent to Claude is processed under Anthropic's API terms. Anthropic does not use API-submitted data to train its models by default. For Anthropic's full data handling and retention policies, see the Anthropic Privacy Policy.

VaultLister does not store the content of AI responses beyond your current session unless you save a generated listing to your inventory.

Opting out of AI features

All AI features in VaultLister are optional. You can use the platform without ever triggering an AI call. To disable AI features entirely, go to Settings → AI & Automation in your account and toggle off AI assistance.

Disabling AI features does not affect cross-listing, inventory management, automations, or any other core functionality.

AI accuracy and human review

AI-generated content (titles, descriptions, price suggestions) is a starting point, not a final output. We recommend reviewing all AI suggestions before publishing a listing. VaultLister does not guarantee the accuracy of AI-generated content.

If you encounter incorrect, harmful, or unexpected AI output, please contact us so we can investigate and improve.

Typography

VaultLister uses Inter as its primary typeface across all UI and marketing materials.

  • Headings: Inter, weight 700–800, letter-spacing −0.04em
  • Body: Inter, weight 400–500, line-height 1.6–1.7
  • Labels / caps: Inter, weight 700, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.07em
  • Monospace (code): system monospace stack

Inter is available via Google Fonts or rsms.me/inter.

Logo Usage — Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Use the logo with adequate clear space (at least the height of the "V" on all sides)
  • Use on white, light grey, or dark (#18181B) backgrounds
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or skew
  • Use the SVG for print and large formats
  • Use the amber gradient lockup on brand-accent hero areas

Don't

  • Don't recolour or apply effects to the logo
  • Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't add drop shadows, outlines, or bevel effects
  • Don't rotate or mirror the logo
  • Don't use the logo to imply endorsement without written permission
