VaultLister vs Flyp

VaultLister gives you full control of your reselling business. Flyp takes a commission to sell for you.

Get Started Free

Flyp is a consignment-based platform that connects sellers with professional consigners who photograph, list, and sell items on your behalf, taking a percentage of each sale. It is a managed service model -- not a cross-listing or automation tool. VaultLister is the opposite: you stay in control, list to 9 marketplaces yourself, and keep 100% of your proceeds, starting completely free.
