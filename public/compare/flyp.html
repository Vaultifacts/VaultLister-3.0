VaultLister vs Flyp

Both tools cross-list and automate Poshmark sharing. See where VaultLister goes further.

Get Started Free

Flyp is a free cross-listing and Poshmark automation tool available as a browser extension (100-day free trial, then $9/mo). It supports crosslisting to Poshmark, eBay, Mercari, Facebook Marketplace, Depop, and Etsy, and includes Poshmark-specific features like closet sharing, auto-offers, and follow-back. However, it does not offer AI listing generation, inventory management, financial reporting, or P&L tracking. VaultLister covers all of that, across 6 live marketplaces (3 coming soon), starting completely free.
