VaultLister vs Primelister

See how VaultLister compares to Primelister for Poshmark automation and multi-platform reselling.

Start Free Trial

Primelister is a Poshmark-focused automation tool offering auto-sharing, re-listing, and automated offer sending to likers. It is effective for growing a Poshmark closet and offers crosslisting to 8 platforms on its $49.99/mo plan, but does not generate listing content or provide any financial reporting. Sellers should also be aware that Primelister's extension uploads marketplace session cookies to api.primelister.com and stores Poshmark credentials on its servers to power its automation. VaultLister includes equivalent Poshmark automation alongside support for 8 additional marketplaces, AI-powered listing generation, and complete business financials — all through a browser-session model that never captures marketplace cookies or credentials, starting free.

Why choose VaultLister over Primelister?

  • VaultLister is free -- Primelister charges $25/mo for Poshmark-only automation, or $49.99/mo to unlock crosslisting to 8 platforms.
