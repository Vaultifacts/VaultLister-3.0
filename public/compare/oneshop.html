VaultLister vs OneShop

See how VaultLister compares to OneShop for cross-listing, AI tools, and seller automation.

Start Free Trial

OneShop is a cross-listing and automation platform that supports five marketplaces including Poshmark, Mercari, eBay, Depop, and Tradesy. Paid plans start at $45/mo with a 7-day free trial. It focuses on closet automation and cross-listing but does not include AI listing generation or financial reporting. VaultLister covers 7 live marketplaces (4 coming soon), includes Claude AI listing generation, full P&L reports, and a free tier that never expires. Sellers should also be aware that OneShop relays marketplace session cookies to its own servers to power automation features.

Why choose VaultLister over OneShop?

  • VaultLister is free forever -- OneShop requires a $45/mo subscription after a 7-day trial, one of the highest prices in the category.
  • Nine marketplaces versus five -- VaultLister lists where OneShop cannot, with more platforms on the roadmap.
  • Claude AI generates complete listings from photos at no extra cost -- OneShop has no AI listing generation.
  • VaultLister uses a browser-session architecture that never captures marketplace credentials -- OneShop relays marketplace session cookies to its own servers to perform automation.

More marketplaces, Claude AI, P&L reports, and a privacy-first architecture -- all free to start.
